Rappold

Christophe

Researcher in nuclear and hadron physics

Brief summary

My scientific research has been focused on experimental study of hypernuclear matter, along with statistical data analysis, optimal design and other numerical optimization problems. I have one of major role in the HypHI collaboration, which aimed at performing hypernuclear spectroscopy by means of heavy-ion induced reactions. I have as well participated in the A1, η-PRiME and SuperFRS collaborations. Currently, I am author in more than 20 publications, and 30 conference proceedings of international conferences.
